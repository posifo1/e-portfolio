Security and Risk Management

This module explores methodologies for identifying, analysing, and treating information security risks while aligning controls with business objectives.

Learning Outcomes

  • Apply risk management frameworks (ISO/IEC 27005, NIST SP 800-30/37).
  • Evaluate governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) practices.
  • Perform risk assessments and propose mitigation strategies.
