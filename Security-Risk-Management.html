Security and Risk Management

This module explores methodologies for identifying, analysing, and treating information security risks while aligning controls with business objectives.

Reflection

The Security and Risk Management module helped me understand that effective security depends on both governance and technology. Applying ISO 27005 and NIST frameworks allowed me to systematically identify and evaluate threats, leading to actionable risk treatment plans. Through our case studies, I learned to balance technical control recommendations with organisational priorities. This process improved my analytical and communication skills, and reinforced the importance of aligning security with business objectives.
